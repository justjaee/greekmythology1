Who's Athena?

One of the most popular greek Gods is Athena, born from Zeus & Metis! It is said that Zeus had an affair with Metis, heard that she would have his son (who would soon overthrow him.) Zeus wanted to stop this from happening so he swallowed her. Athena then was born inside & sprung from his head, fully grown & fully suited! She became the Goddess of Widsom & Battle Strategy. She's a fan favorite because she was female who was strong & wise during a time where women were considered property! No wonder she's a fan favorite.
